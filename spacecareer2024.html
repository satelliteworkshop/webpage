Space Career Launchpad on 18.1.2024 @ 15:00

Winter Satellite Workshop brings once a year space technology, space science and remote sensing to Otaniemi. This is the largest space event in Finland, Scandinavia and Baltics. Use this opportunity and launch your carrees to space industry. The event consists of more than 250 conference presentations and over 30 company booths at EXPO area. The EXPO provides to students first hand access to European space industry and space institutions in Finland. Come and ask about thesis topic, summerwork or negotiate your first serious contract. The space is full of opportunities. Check also out our speakers and program. The program includes European space leaders and also an astronaut.
