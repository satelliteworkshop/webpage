Oral Sessions

  • Oral presentations are 13 minutes long, with an additional 2 minutes allocated for speaker transitions.
  • Questions and answers will be possible only if time allows. For longer discussions, please use the coffee breaks.
  • There are no specific requirements for presentation graphics.
  • All oral presentations will be broadcast online to registered attendees during the conference.
  • Presentations must be uploaded to the designated computer via a provided link. We recommend uploading them at least one hour before your scheduled presentation.
  • Presentations can be linked to the program upon request.
  • Only PowerPoint and PDF formats are supported.
  • Use of personal laptops for presentations is not possible.
  • Upload links will be available during next week.
