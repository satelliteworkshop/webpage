Finland executive Earth observation breakfast seminar 2023

RSVP Agenda

Dear Guests,

New Space, a new era of space-based capabilities to improve life on Earth enabled by small satellite and cost-effective launch services, calls for deeper collaboration between governments, the private sector, society, and politics. Two Finnish Earth observation companies, ICEYE and Kuva Space are already leading the way in providing world-class space-borne solutions and data services to tackle climate change impacts and make our world safer.

The war in Ukraine has spurred defense organizations across Europe and the US to utilize commercial satellite data more pervasively, paving the way for New Defence - a novel collaboration between commercial and public entities in the defense sector.

New Space companies like ICEYE and Kuva Space are poised to deliver accurate and daily insights that are not feasible with the traditional space satellite's legacy technologies. The new space companies are delivering new and timely data services that promise more transparency and accountability.

As decision-makers, you play a crucial role in shaping the future of Finland. The use of Earth observation technology has the potential to impact various industries and inform decision-making greatly. Investment and strategic interest in Earth observation and the New Space ecosystem at the governmental level are vital for the growth of this industry in Finland.

This exclusive breakfast event will allow Finnish decision-makers to learn about the Earth observation ecosystem in Finland and the meaningful opportunities that lie ahead for Finland. We hope to see you at this event, and together accelerate the Finnish New Space ecosystem growth. Please confirm your participation here.

 

Jarkko Antila
CEO, Kuva Space

Pekka Laurila
Co-founder, ICEYE

About Kuva Space

Kuva Space is a satellite and AI company. The company's satellite constellation, equipped with a state-of-the-art hyperspectral imaging sensor, enables frequent and reliable monitoring of the Earth's surface for various applications, such as agriculture, defense, and environmental monitoring. Kuva Space was founded in 2016 and was known as Reaktor Space Lab before its rebranding in 2021.

www.kuvaspace.com

About ICEYE

ICEYE is a Finnish company that develops and produces synthetic-aperture radar (SAR) satellite technology. Its satellites provide high-resolution imaging, even in challenging conditions. They are used in fields such as disaster response, environmental monitoring, and defense. The company was founded in 2014 and has launched more than 20 satellites.

www.iceye.com

About Finnish Satellite Workshop (FSW)

The Finnish Satellite Workshop is the biggest new space meet-up in Finland, Scandinavia and the Baltic countries. It brings together space tech specialists, scientists and students to discuss current topics in a rapidly developing space field. With well over 150 abstracts featuring latest EO and space development, FSW is set to present you the latest in the new space industry.
