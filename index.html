Satellite Workshop and Space Business EXPO

The Winter Satellite Workshop is the largest New Space meeting in Finland, Scandinavia and the Baltic countries. It brings together space tech specialists, scientists, and students to discuss current topics in a rapidly developing space field. The scope of the workshop set on New Space, small satellite missions, small satellite platform development, space science and instruments, Earth Observation and sustainable utilization of space. In 2024, the event had more than 1200 registrations, over 260 presentations and 35 space companies and partners.
