Finnish Satellite Workshop 2020

with Finnish Remote Sensing Days

20-22 January 2020, HELSINKI, FINLAND

Technical Program

About The Event

The Finnish Satellite Workshop is the biggest New Space meeting in Finland, but also in Scandinavia and the Baltic countries. It brings together space tech specialists, scientists and students to discuss current topics in a rapidly developing space field. The scope is set on New Space, small satellite missions, small satellite platform development, space science missions and instruments, Earth Observation and sustainable utilization of space, not forgetting also about collaboration and politics.

This year's event is arranged together with the Finnish Remote Sensing Club and it contains also Finnish Remote Sensing Days.

We also provide the possibility for companies to increase their visibility by sponsoring and presenting their products in the technical exhibition area. Please register your company to the event.

The workshop format is relaxed, focusing on content and collaboration. Several workshops and side events are arranged along the happening. Please propose your talk by submitting a small simple abstract. You can also propose workshop topics to be discussed during the meeting.

The happening is free of charge for pre-registered participants, including the social events and coffee breaks. The happening is also an excellent forum for companies, who can guarantee their visibility by sponsoring the event and reserving booth area.
