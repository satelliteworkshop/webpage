Finnish Satellite Workshop 2023

The event has received over 70 abstracts about latest developments in space technology and space research. The program is published soon!

The Finnish Satellite Workshop is the biggest New Space meeting in Finland, Scandinavia and the Baltic countries. It brings together space tech specialists, scientists and students to discuss current topics in a rapidly developing space field. The scope is set on New Space, small satellite missions, small satellite platform development, space science missions and instruments, Earth Observation and sustainable utilization of space, not forgetting also about collaboration and politics.

Finnish Remote Sensing Days

The event has received over 50 abstracts on latest advancements in Earth Observation topics. The full program will be published soon.

The Finnish Remote Sensing Club represents the community of Remote Sensing specialists and professionls in Finland. Over 25 yers, the Finnish Remote Sensing Club has arranged a workshop, called Finnish Remote Sensing Days. The event has brought together specialists all over Scandinavia and Baltic states to discuss the latest advancements in the fields and developing ideas for next space instruments and funding applications. Since 2020 Remote Sening Days have been organized together with Satellite Workshop.

XXXVI Finnish URSI Convention on Radio Science

The event has received over 30 abstracts on latest advenaces in radio science. The detailed program will be published soon.

The International Union of Radio Science (Union Radio Scientifique Internationale, URSI) was founded in 1919 to stimulate and coordinate international research, applications and communication in the field of radio science. The Finnish National Committee (Member Committee) consists of 15 institutional members, mainly representing universities, research institutes and scientific organizations. The main national event of the Finnish National Committee is the National Convention on Radio Science.

Fore more information pleas see Finnish URSI homepage . The XXXVI Finnish URSI Convention on Radio Science is arranged together with Finnish Satellite Workshop and Remote Sensing Days 2023.
